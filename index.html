ABOUT ME

My name is Java Monsta. I’m a professional graphic artist and web developer, based in Texas. I began my career in print media in 1998, working as a graphic designer for national publishers such as Tribune Media and Gannett. In 2010, I pivoted into software engineering after studying web development at New York University.

Now I’m a full stack developer who can articulate my ideas in an effective, compelling way. Whether it’s through a responsive website, mobile app design or back-end coding, my experience working with a variety of technologies has helped me excel in environments that are often fast-paced, detail oriented and involve diverse tasks. My approach to every client is the same: provide creative and cost effective solutions that appeal to the target market. I love providing value that accomplishes personal and business goals.
